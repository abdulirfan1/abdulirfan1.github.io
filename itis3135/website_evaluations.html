Website Evaluations

The Big Ugly Website

This website is intentionally designed to demonstrate poor design principles. It features a chaotic layout, flashing elements, and excessive colors, making it an overwhelming experience for the user.

  • Contrast: Lacks balance with overly bright colors and distracting visuals that make text hard to read.
  • Repetition: Minimal repetition, leading to a scattered look that lacks unity.
  • Alignment: Poor alignment, with elements appearing randomly placed, making it difficult to navigate.
  • Proximity: Items are not logically grouped, creating a confusing experience for users trying to locate content.

Apple

Apple's website is known for its clean and minimalist design, providing a highly organized and visually appealing experience that reflects the brand's identity.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast with a clean, white background and high-quality images that focus attention on products.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and layout elements that create a unified and professional look.
  • Alignment: Excellent alignment with a well-organized layout, making it easy for users to follow the structure.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped effectively, creating an intuitive flow and improving readability.
